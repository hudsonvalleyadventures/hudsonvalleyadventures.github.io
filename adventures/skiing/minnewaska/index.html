Cross country skiing at Lake Minnewaska

Work in progress!

Lake Minnewaska is a popular cross country skiing destination with many miles of trails and incredible views from the Shawangunk Ridge. There is a fee for parking. Ask for a trail map from the parking attendants. We recommend skiing to Lake Awosting via the Castle Point or Hamilton Point Carriage Roads (for experience skiers) or via the Upper Awasting Carriage Road (for less experienced skiers). We also recommend skiing around Lake Awosting if you have time, though the trails aren't always groomed so be prepared for skiing through powder! Wider skis such as cross country downhill skis can be useful for skiing around Lake Awosting when the trail isn't groomed.

See the NY government website for helpful information like trail conditions and maps.
